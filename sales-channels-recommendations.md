## TikTok Shop — action plan

This is the highest-leverage missing channel. Setup:

1. **Create TikTok Seller account**: https://seller.tiktok.com
2. **Link to Shopify**: Shopify admin → Apps → search "TikTok Shop" → install
3. **Verify business documents**: government ID, business registration (Canada-based, so Canadian docs)
4. **Connect product catalog**: bulk-import 6,155 products
5. **Set shipping**: TikTok Shop requires defined shipping templates. Use Canada Post or similar.
6. **Wait for approval**: TikTok Shop seller approval takes 1-3 business days

Once approved:
- Products surface in TikTok Shop tab
- Eligible products appear in TikTok videos when creators search
- Puchica can run TikTok Shop ads (different from regular TikTok Ads)

The catch: **TikTok Shop is more strict on certain product categories**. The 98 intimate products we just recategorized may need category-by-category approval for TikTok Shop listing. Sexual Wellness products may be restricted or banned from TikTok Shop entirely.
